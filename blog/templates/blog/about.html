Amigo Blog

About Us

Welcome to our blog, your go-to destination for everything related to the Latin community in the UK. Created with love and passion by Juan Fernando Pinzon Moreno, a proud Latin immigrant, our mission is to share the rich and diverse experiences of Latinos living in the UK, fostering a sense of community, support, and connection.

Our Purpose

Our blog serves as a platform to highlight the vibrant stories and journeys of Latin immigrants in the UK. We aim to provide a space where members of our community can share their personal experiences, challenges, and triumphs. By doing so, we hope to inspire and uplift each other, creating a network of support and solidarity.

Our Mission

  • Building Connections: Creating a network where Latin immigrants can connect, support each other, and share valuable resources.
  • Sharing Stories: Highlighting the unique experiences of Latin individuals in the UK, celebrating our culture and heritage.
  • Providing Resources: Offering practical advice and information to help our community navigate life in the UK.
  • Fostering Unity: Embracing our diverse backgrounds and working together to build a stronger, more connected community.

Meet the Founder

Juan Fernando Pinzon Moreno
Juan is a passionate advocate for the Latin community in the UK. As a Latin immigrant himself, he understands the challenges and opportunities that come with adapting to a new country. Through this blog, Juan aims to create a welcoming and inclusive space where Latin voices can be heard, stories can be shared, and lasting connections can be made.

Our Vision

Our vision is to see a thriving, united Latin community in the UK where every individual feels valued and supported. We believe in the power of storytelling and networking to bring about positive change and to build bridges between cultures.
